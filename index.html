Crystal Collector

How to play:

  • You will be given a random target number at the start of the game.
  • There are four crystals for you to click from which are given random hidden values
  • The hidden gem values will be added to your total score.
  • Your goal is to reach the target number without surpassing it
  • The game will reset after every win/loss, and the crystal values will be randomized again.
